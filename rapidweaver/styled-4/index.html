Meet Clare




I love what I do!

Seeing clients grow in confidence gives me a real buzz!

I came into the Coaching Profession after receiving coaching myself when I desperately needed a new career path. Because I had personally experienced just how much coaching transformed my life and well being, I decided to encourage and support others to take a risk and be happy - and to find a new and brighter direction in their lives.

My company name Clarity in Your Life illustrates my approach - what you see is what you get! All very straightforward: My clients develop a much clearer idea of where they’re going when we work together.

I believe that as a Coach I should walk the walk as they say:
• I set up a business in Training and Coaching in the UK at the start of the recession (2007) Crazy times but was very successful
• I was then in my mid 50’s. Scary step but incredibly exciting
• Emigrated to Australia in 2011 to be with my son and little family. One of my BEST decisions ever!
• I have developed a range of clients here as a self-employed Leadership and Management Trainer

This is all down to coaching - taking decisive action - getting organised - loving the process!

My life is a Work In Progress – and always will be.
