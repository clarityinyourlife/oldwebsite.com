My Training

I’m a very curious kind of person!

I’ve always been passionate about personal development and growth. I’ve read so many books, listened to audios, attended seminars and searched the internet for anything that could inspire, empower and encourage me to create my own success and well being.

I did a great deal of my coaching training in the UK and knew a little bit about the Law of Attraction. Coming to Australia changed all that! Joining the Inspired Spirit Coaching Academy has been truly amazing as I found a comprehensive amalgamation of innovative coaching methods, practical business tips and techniques, spiritual processes and a lot of common sense in one fabulous training and certification program.
My teachers included Sandy Forster, Director of the Inspired Spirit Coaching Academy and international Mentor of the Year.

Drawing on these skills and my knowledge of different self-empowerment exercises, I work with clients to help them make changes that produce real and lasting results.
I am passionate about sharing that knowledge with others so you too can enjoy a life of increased confidence, loving relationships, vibrant health that comes from creating your life the way YOU want it to be and achieving your goals and dreams.

I hope you will join me either as a coaching client or a student at the Inspired Spirit Coaching Academy. Please email me any questions and I will be happy to follow up with you.

Wishing you Clarity in Your Life,

Clare Coffield
